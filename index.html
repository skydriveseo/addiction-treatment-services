Some Tried and Tested Addiction Treatment Services

Individuals who are seeking addiction treatment and want to get their life back on track can opt for various rehab centers out there. These are the most popular choice among those who want to get rid of any addictions through various treatments and programs.

Even one doesn't have to go through the process alone. A good support network can be a big help. These rehab centers actually provide that help to the addicted ones so that they can come out of their addiction and lead a new life. They are typically private facilities that offer a variety of programs, including outpatient, inpatient, and residential treatment.

There are many addiction treatment services available today. Here are some of the most popular ones:

  • Outpatient Programs: These are the most common types of drug rehab. They offer a range of therapy and counseling sessions, as well as group activities, for those who have completed inpatient treatment.
  • Intensive Outpatient Programs: Intensive outpatient programs offer a more intense version of outpatient drug rehab. They tend to require that patients commit to staying at the facility for a longer period of time, and may include longer therapy sessions and more intensive group counseling.
  • Residential Treatment: Residential treatment centers provide more intensive care than intensive outpatient programs. Patients generally live in a facility while they are receiving treatment, though they may be allowed limited freedom during certain hours each day so they can attend school or work.
  • Inpatient Drug Rehabs: Inpatient drug rehabs are similar to residential facilities but require that patients stay there for an extended period of time (at least 30 days). This type of program is often used when someone has only been abusing drugs for a short amount of time (such as several weeks), or when someone's behaviors have become too dangerous for them to live at home.

Most rehab facilities require patients to pay fees upfront before they begin treatment. These fees tend to be higher than those charged at less-established facilities, but they may also include more services and amenities than those offered by less-established programs. Some rehabs also offer scholarships or payment plans that help clients pay their bills while they are in treatment.

Apart from these, some addiction treatment centers offer the following therapies:

  • Individual Substance Abuse Counselling
  • Group Therapy
  • Family Therapy
  • Motivational Enhancement Therapy (MET)

This is basically a 6-day program in which participants learn to identify and manage their own addictive behavior. Participants receive individual counseling, group therapy, and individual counseling. Participants also participate in motivational enhancement therapy (MET). MET is a 12-step program that uses an Alcoholics Anonymous approach to addiction recovery.

In addition to these, medication along with certain behavioral therapies also helps in decreasing an individual’s addictive behavior.
